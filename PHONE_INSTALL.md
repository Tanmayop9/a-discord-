## ЁЯУ▒ English Instructions

### Simple 3-Step Installation (No PC Needed!)

#### Step 1: Download APK
1. Open your phone browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
2. Go to: https://github.com/Tanmayop9/a-discord-/releases
3. Tap on the latest release
4. Tap on the APK file to download
5. Wait for download to complete

#### Step 2: Enable Installation from Unknown Sources
- **Android 8.0 and above:**
  1. Go to Settings
  2. Tap on Apps & notifications
  3. Tap on Advanced тЖТ Special app access
  4. Tap on Install unknown apps
  5. Select your browser (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
  6. Enable "Allow from this source"

- **Android 7.1 and below:**
  1. Go to Settings
  2. Tap on Security
  3. Enable "Unknown sources"
  4. Tap OK on the warning

#### Step 3: Install the App
1. Open your phone's Downloads folder or notification
2. Tap on the downloaded APK file
3. Tap "Install"
4. Wait for installation to complete
5. Tap "Open" or find app in your app drawer

#### Step 4: Use the App
1. Open "Discord Voice Mod"
2. Grant microphone permission (tap "Allow")
3. Choose your voice effect
4. Tap "Start Recording"
5. Enjoy your modified voice!
